About us:

Your car repair experts

Morgatech Auto is a car repair shop located in Beltsville, Maryland, near Washington D.C., along Ammendale Road. Serving not only Beltsville but also College Park, Greenbelt, Laurel, and Hyattsville, we offer quality and cost-friendly car repair services for all makes and models of cars. Our team specializes in european car repairs such as Volkswagen repairs, BMW repairs, Range rover repairs, Mercedes benz repairs, Audi repairs and more.

Expert services for your car

At Morgatech Auto Repair, we provide a comprehensive range of services for most makes and models, and local models too. Whether it's routine maintenance or more complex repairs like engine diagnostics, oil changes, brake repairs, and tire services, we ensure your vehicle is always road-ready. Our strong suit is foreign car repairs where we specialize in european car repairs. We are a family owned business doing our best to provide you with expert car services that you can trust.
