Affordable transmission services in Beltsville, MD

Welcome to morgatech auto transmission shop where we perform expert repairs of the differential, input and output shaft or even entire transmssion rebuild should you need one. However, we know that a stitch in time saves nine which is why we are keen to identify when you may need to replace some key transmission components such as the seal, mount, torque converters among others as early attention can help lower the costs you may incur. Here are three transmission repair jobs you should get done often for best car health.

Transmission fluid replacement and transmission flush

Transmission fluid should be replaced every 30,000 - 60,000 as over time it can break down and become contaminated and therefore be less efficient at cooling and lubricating the car transmission. During a transmission fluid replacement, the fluid is removed and any filters are replaced before filling up the system with new fluid. If the transmission fluid is very badly degraded, a machine is used to thoroughly drain out all the transmission fluid and replace it with new one. this is known as transmission flush.

Transmission band adjustment.

Many automatic transmission cars have a transmission band which may become worn out after prolonged use affecting it is recommended that you have your transmission checked every 60,000 -100,000 miles.

Drivetrain maintenance/ driveshaft repair

We perform driveshaft repair service at morgatech auto. Having a properly maintained drivetrain can ensure your car transfers power effcicently from the engine to the wheels.

Transmission care tips

In order to ensure your transmission stays working efficiently, our experts in the morgatech transmission shop have provided the following tips:

  • Regular transmission fluid changes (30 - 60 thousand miles)
  • Avoid overloading; try to stick to the manufacturer guidelines on how much weight the car can carry or tow.
  • Use the right transmission fluid for your vehicle as stipulated in your vehicle mannual.
  • Regular inspections.
  • Avoid aggressive driving which could cause your transmission to wear out faster.
