Exhaust Services

Your car exhaust system serves to divert the exhaust fumes from your engine safely to the environment. A faulty exhaust system poses potential risks to your engine, to you and your passengers and to your environment when harmful pollutants are not filtered out of the exhaust fumes. To avoid all of this, you can trust the mechanics at Morgatech auto in Beltsville, Maryland to have your exhaust system working without hiccups.

Muffler Repair and maintenance

The muffler is the part of your exhaust that is responsible for reducing the amount of noise released by your car. It is important to keep it in proper shape to avoid infringing on noise reduction laws in your area and to limit unwanted noise for your own sake. At Morgatech auto, we perform muffler replacement and repair where necessary to ensure that your experience with your vehicle is the best it can be.

Catalytic converter repair and replacement

A catalytic converter is a unit that is installed in most cars to reduce their emissions of harmful pollutants such as hydrocarbons, Oxides of nitrogen and carbon monoxide. It is important to understand how essential this component is especially in this day and age when pollution poses a threat to environmental health. Our mechanics at Morgatech Auto will gladly repair your damaged catalytic converter or replace it if the need should arise to ensure that you do not harm the environment every time you get in your car.
